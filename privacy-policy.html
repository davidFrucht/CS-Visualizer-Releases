CS Visualizer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

CS Visualizer is a local desktop application and browser extension that visualizes code directly on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

CS Visualizer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • Your code is processed entirely on your local machine and is never sent to any external server.
  • No usage analytics or telemetry are collected.
  • No account or registration is required.
  • The browser extension stores only a single on/off preference in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

AI Assistant (Optional)

If you choose to use the optional AI Assistant feature, it connects to a local Ollama instance running on your own machine. No data is sent to any third-party AI service unless you explicitly configure one.

Third-Party Services

CS Visualizer does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

CS Visualizer does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. As no data is collected from any user, this application is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated date.
